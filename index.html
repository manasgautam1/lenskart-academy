Who is Lenskart Academy for?

Academy of Retail

A school for retail professionals which runs a unique, intensive training program that's designed in-sync with the needs of modern, tech-based retail. Learn progressive skill-sets with a razor-sharp focus on customer service and collaboration.


Whom is it for: Experienced professionals and freshers who have completed class 12 or 10+3 diploma

Academy of Optometry

A school for optometrists that transforms you into a world-class professional. The well-crafted training program blends technical and soft-skills, connecting academics with the industry.


Whom is it for: Those with prior knowledge of optometry, including a Diploma in Optometry, Bachelors in Optometry, Masters in Optometry, or B.Sc. in Optometry.
